Why More Advice Hasn't Worked

Aapko teesra consultant nahi chahiye.
Aapko doosri nazar chahiye.

Every consultant you've hired looked at the same material: revenue, funnel, team structure, cost lines, systems. They weren't wrong. That analysis is real and it's necessary.

But it can only see what's recorded.

Here's the case it can't explain.

A founder whose expansion decisions fail — three times, three years apart, each time for a different reason on paper.

Root cause analysis will produce three separate explanations. All three will be technically correct. None of them will explain why it keeps happening.

◈

Because that isn't a business problem in the way your P&L defines one. It's a pattern. And patterns don't show up in a spreadsheet — they show up in timing.
